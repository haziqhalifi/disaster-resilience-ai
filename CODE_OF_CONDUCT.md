# Code Of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a friendly, safe, and inclusive environment for all contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Focus on the project, not personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks, intimidation, or trolling.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments and contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately through repository contact channels.
